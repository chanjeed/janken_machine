Press BLUE button to show score bar graph.
Press RED button to start rock,paper,scissors game.
Press YELLOW button to reset score to zero.

After game start, when finished counting 3-2-1, press button to choose shape to play (BLUE:Rock,RED:Scissors,YELLOW:Paper).
If you did not choose any shape, Janken Machine will start counting 3-2-1 again.
You can also choose shape by putting your hand above camera.

Then, this machine will show computer's play and show game result (Win / Lose / Draw).
You can play game again as much as you want by press RED button!
